About Cooking Mama

Our mission is to be the smartest
and most helpful food platform in existence

We're fulfilling this mission by improving life in the kitchen
for millions of home cooks around the world.

We help people discover what to eat based on personal preferences and data

Our personalized food discovery system analyzes your tastes, preferences, and data to suggest meal options that fit your needs, ensuring that your dining choices are always a perfect match.

We are building big data for food

By leveraging big data and advanced technology, we analyze millions of recipes to understand ingredients, cuisines, and dietary preferences. Our goal is to provide tailored meal suggestions based on your unique tastes and dietary needs.

Building a platform for food discovery

We are committed to building a comprehensive recipe discovery platform that not only helps users find new recipes but also provides a seamless experience for those seeking healthier, quicker, and more personalized food choices.
